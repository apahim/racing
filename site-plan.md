## I. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: THE MISSION
**Pahim Racing** is the intersection of elite software engineering and competitive motorsport. Founded by a Senior Principal Engineer at Red Hat, the team treats the **2027 Tillotson T4 Ireland Championship** not as a sporting hobby, but as a complex system optimization problem. 

Our goal is to demonstrate that **professional discipline, open-source philosophy, and data-driven iteration** can outperform traditional "intuitive" racing models.

## II. THE HUMAN STACK: "SYSTEM REFACTOR -30KG"
In motorsport, the driver is the primary processing unit. A suboptimal unit introduces latency and physical "technical debt."

### 1. Hardware Optimization
- **Mass Reduction:** Successfully executed a **30kg weight refactor** (Power-to-Weight Optimization).
- **Physics Impact:** In a 165kg (Kart + Driver) class, a 30kg reduction represents an efficiency gain of ~18%.
- **Biometric Uptime:** Focus on zone-2 cardiovascular endurance and neck-load conditioning to maintain "Peak CPU" performance during 15-minute high-G finals.

### 2. Cognitive Architecture
- **Decision Latency:** Training for rapid "Overtake/Defend" heuristics under 140bpm heart rate stress.
- **Resilience:** Applying Root Cause Analysis (RCA) to on-track incidents to prevent recursive errors.

## III. THE SOFTWARE STACK: BOXBOX TELEMETRY
**Location:** [boxbox.pahim.org](https://boxbox.pahim.org)
**Source:** [GitHub: apahim/boxbox](https://github.com/apahim/boxbox)

While competitors rely on anecdotal "track feel," Pahim Racing utilizes a custom-built telemetry pipeline.

### 1. Pipeline Overview
- **Data Acquisition:** GoPro Hero metadata extraction (GPMF).
- **Processing:** Python-based parser to isolate GPS, Accelerometer (G-force), and Gyroscope streams.
- **Analysis:** Delta-time comparison between "Ideal Lap" and "Actual Lap."
- **Visualization:** High-contrast telemetry overlays for post-session debugging.

### 2. Feature Roadmap (2026-2027)
- **v2.1:** Integration of OBD2/CAN bus data for engine temp and RPM monitoring.
- **v2.2:** Predictive "Ideal Line" modeling using historical track data.
- **v2.3:** Real-time dashboard for pit-to-driver technical feedback.

## IV. THE HARDWARE: TILLOTSON T4 (MONO-BRAND PARITY)
The Tillotson T4 was selected specifically for its **Technical Constraints**.

- **Engine:** Tillotson TPP-225RS (Sealed for parity).
- **Chassis:** T4-C1 (Mono-brand).
- **Strategic Value:** Because the hardware is standardized, the "Win Condition" is shifted entirely to **Driver Optimization and Data Analysis.** It is the ultimate "Control Environment" for an engineer.
