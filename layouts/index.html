Amador Pahim

A lifelong passion for racing, shaped by growing up watching the great Senna, never left. Karting remained a constant through the years, but the discovery of Ireland's Tillotson T4 Championship, a sealed-engine series where engineering discipline outweighs budget, was the catalyst to return to serious competition.
