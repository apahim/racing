THE PROGRAM

// The Mission

System Optimization

Pahim Racing exists at the intersection of elite software engineering and competitive motorsport. The 2027 Tillotson T4 Ireland Championship is a complex system optimization problem with measurable inputs, testable hypotheses, and quantifiable outputs.

The objective: demonstrate that professional discipline, open-source methodology, and data-driven iteration can outperform traditional intuition-based racing models. Every variable—from driver mass to cornering trajectory—is a parameter to be measured, analyzed, and optimized.
