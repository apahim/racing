## The Mission

Pahim Racing exists at the intersection of elite software engineering and competitive motorsport. The 2027 Tillotson T4 Ireland Championship is not a sporting hobby — it is a complex system optimization problem with measurable inputs, testable hypotheses, and quantifiable outputs.

The objective is direct: demonstrate that **professional discipline, open-source methodology, and data-driven iteration** can outperform traditional intuition-based racing models. Every variable — from driver mass to cornering trajectory — is treated as a parameter to be measured, analyzed, and optimized.

## Human System Optimization

In motorsport, the driver is the primary processing unit. A suboptimal unit introduces latency and accumulates physical technical debt.

### Hardware Refactor: -30kg

A 30kg mass reduction was executed as a power-to-weight optimization. In a class where the combined kart-and-driver mass is approximately 165kg, removing 30kg represents an **~18% efficiency gain** — a delta that translates directly to acceleration, braking distance, and cornering load.

### Biometric Uptime

Peak performance during 15-minute high-G finals demands sustained cardiovascular output under stress. The conditioning protocol targets zone-2 endurance and neck-load resilience to maintain consistent "CPU performance" when it matters most.

### Cognitive Architecture

- **Decision Latency:** Rapid "Overtake/Defend" heuristic execution under 140bpm heart rate stress
- **Resilience:** Root Cause Analysis applied to on-track incidents to prevent recursive errors
- **Consistency:** Targeting sub-0.1s variance in lap-to-lap delta

## The Control Environment

The Tillotson T4 was selected for its **technical constraints**, not despite them.

- **Engine:** Tillotson TPP-225RS — sealed for parity
- **Chassis:** T4-C1 — mono-brand specification
- **Strategic Value:** Standardized hardware shifts the win condition entirely to **driver optimization and data analysis**

When the machine is identical, the only differentiator is the system around it. For an engineer, this is the ideal control environment.

## 2027 Championship Roadmap

| Phase | Timeline | Primary KPI |
